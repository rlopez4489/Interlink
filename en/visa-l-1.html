L-1 visa for investors

L-1A and L-1B visas are available to intra-corporate transferees in managerial positions or who have specialized knowledge.

Requirements to apply for the L-1A and L-1B visa

The L-1A and L-1B visas can be granted when an employer files a petition to obtain authorization for a qualified employee to work and live in the United States.
The L-1A visa is for intra-corporate transferees who work in managerial or executive positions at a company located outside the United States.
The L-1B visa is for intra-corporate transferees who work in positions that require specialized knowledge.
Foreign employers seeking to send an employee to the United States as an executive or manager to establish a new branch must have an office, the employee has served one (1) consecutive year in the role, the new branch must fund the position executive for one (1) year.
