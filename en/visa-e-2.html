E-2 visa for investors

The United States offers several investment options and varieties of visas to which you can apply, one of the simplest and most requested options is the E-2 visa.

Requirements to apply for the E-2 visa

The investor must go to the United States to develop and run the company. For this reason the investment cannot be marginal, but must be made in an operating company.
The investor must have a nationality of a country that maintains a Free Trade and Navigation Agreement with the United States.
A substantial and sufficient investment must be made to ensure the successful operation of the business. We recommend an investment of $120,000 to $150,000 USD.
It must generate income significantly greater than what is necessary to support the investor and their family as well as generate a positive impact on the US economy.
The investor must have control of the funds, at least 50%, and the investment must be committed.

Benefits of applying for the E-2 visa

It allows you to enter and work in the United States for your own business.
Your spouse and unmarried children (under the age of 21) also qualify to reside in the United States as E-2 dependents.
Your spouse may apply for an employment permit to look for work in any company in the United States and may even work in their own business.
Your children under the age of 21 (single), will be able to study in public/private schools or universities in the United States.
The E-2 visa is relatively simple to apply. It is a temporary visa, however it can be extended indefinitely as long as the company continues to produce.
